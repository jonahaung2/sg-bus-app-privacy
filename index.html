Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SG Bus Arrival (“the App”) handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

The App may access the following information:

  • Precise Location: Used to identify nearby bus stops and provide relevant bus arrival times.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your location is used solely to:

  • Detect nearby bus stops
  • Display real-time bus arrival information

3. Data Storage

  • Your location data is not stored on any server
  • All data is used temporarily and on-device

4. Data Sharing

  • No personal data is sold or shared
  • Location may be used to request public transport data, without identifying you

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use public transport APIs to retrieve bus arrival timings. These services do not receive personally identifiable information.

6. Your Choices

  • You can disable location access anytime in your device settings
  • Some features may not work without location access

7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
